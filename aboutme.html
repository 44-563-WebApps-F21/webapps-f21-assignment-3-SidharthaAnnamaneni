Aquaman

Aquaman is a 2018 American superhero film based on the DC Comics character of the same name. Distributed by Warner Bros.Aquaman's most widely recognized power is the telepathic ability to communicate with marine life, which he can summon from great distances.The Trident, granted by Poseidon to the rightful ruler and protector of the seas, was indestructible and a very powerful melee weapon, which Aquaman wielded with unmatched skill.
